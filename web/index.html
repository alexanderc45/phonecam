How to use / troubleshooting
1. Keep this phone and the PC running PhoneCamRelay.exe on the same Wi-Fi.
2. Tap Start streaming and allow motion access if asked.
3. In BeamNG press Shift+C (free camera), hold the phone level in landscape like you're filming, and tap Recenter.

No sensor data? Browsers block sensors on plain http:// pages — see the yellow banner fix above.
Camera not moving in game? Make sure the mod is installed (a phoneCamera line appears in the BeamNG console) and you're in free-camera mode.
Drifting? Phone gyros drift over minutes — just tap Recenter again.
